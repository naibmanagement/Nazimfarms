Who We Are

Elevate Your Agro Business with Nazim Farms

Nazim Farms and Agro Allied Limited was incorporated in Nigeria by Nigerians to provide first-class value-added services. We specialise in commodity trading of agricultural produce through the application of the latest technology equipment and quality standard services to our customers’ satisfaction.

From flavorful spices to nutritious soybean seeds, raw cashew nuts to exotic dry hibiscus flower — our diverse portfolio caters to the needs of valued customers worldwide. We take pride in our commitment to excellence, ensuring each product meets the highest standards of quality, freshness and purity.

What We Do

Full-Service Agric Operations

🌾

Crop Production

We produce soya beans, sunflowers, maize and more on our managed farmlands using modern techniques and equipment.

🤝

Outgrower Programme

We partner directly with indigenous farmers in rural communities, providing inputs, training and guaranteed offtake for their harvest.

Processing & Milling

In-house processing of soya oil and agricultural produce using the latest technology to ensure quality, consistency and export readiness.

✈️

Export & Commodity Trading

🚜

Modern Farm Equipment

Access to modern agricultural equipment for planting, harvesting and post-harvest handling — maximising yield and minimising losses.
