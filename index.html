Who We Are

Elevating Nigerian Agriculture to World Markets

Nazim Farms and Agro Allied Limited was incorporated in Nigeria by Nigerians to provide first-class value-added services. We specialise in commodity trading of agricultural produce through the application of the latest technology and quality standard services.

From flavorful spices to nutritious soybean seeds, raw cashew nuts to exotic dry hibiscus flower — our diverse portfolio caters to valued customers worldwide. We ensure each product meets the highest standards of quality, freshness and purity.

What We Do

Full-Service Agric Operations

From farm to export container — we manage the entire chain so your business gets only the best.

🌾

Crop Production

We produce soya beans, sunflowers, maize and more on managed farmlands using modern techniques and equipment.

🤝

Outgrower Programme

We partner with indigenous farmers in rural communities, providing inputs, training and guaranteed offtake for their harvest.

⚙️

Processing & Milling

In-house processing of soya oil and agricultural produce using latest technology to ensure quality and export readiness.

✈️

Export & Commodity Trading

We handle all documentation, certification, logistics and shipping for international buyers across Europe, Asia and the Middle East.

🚜

Modern Farm Equipment

Access to modern agricultural equipment for planting, harvesting and post-harvest handling — maximising yield and minimising losses.

Distribution & Supply
